About

Figure 5 Media is a Los Angeles-based post-production studio specializing in short-form content for brands that demand excellence.

With ten years of experience working with Fortune 500 companies, major tech platforms, and entertainment clients, Figure 5 Media transforms raw footage into polished, compelling narratives that drive results. Our full-service post-production includes editing, color grading, sound design, motion graphics, and delivery—with a focus on quick turnarounds without compromising quality.
