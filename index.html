About

Figure 5 Media is a Los Angeles-based post-production studio with ten years of experience partnering with startups, established brands, and Fortune 500 Companies—specializing in short-form content for brands that demand excellence.

Combining producer-level storytelling with editorial precision, Figure 5 transforms raw footage into polished narratives—streamlining workflow by eliminating the need for detailed scripts or extensive directorial oversight. Our full-service post-production includes editing, color grading, sound design, motion graphics, and delivery—with a focus on quick turnarounds without compromising quality.
